#### Solution to challenge #3
```
telomerase = open("telomerase.txt")

seq = telomerase.read()

for aa in "ACDEFGHKILMNPQRSTVYW":
  aa_count = seq.count(aa)
  aa_freq = aa_count/float(len(seq))
  print aa, round(aa_freq, 3)
```
